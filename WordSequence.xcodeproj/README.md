# Word Sequence

A memory and sequencing game designed to help children develop patience, focus, and sequential thinking skills.

## Background

Word Sequence was created following a discussion with my autistic son's Occupational Therapist. During our conversation, she explained that activities teaching patience and doing things in a particular sequence can significantly help improve focus in carrying out day-to-day activities. 

This game transforms that therapeutic concept into an engaging, colorful experience that makes learning these essential skills fun and rewarding. By remembering and tapping words in the correct order, children practice:

- **Sequential thinking** — understanding that order matters
- **Working memory** — holding multiple items in mind
- **Patience** — waiting for all information before acting
- **Focus** — maintaining attention throughout the task
- **Visual processing** — tracking multiple items on screen
- **Letter and word recognition** — because the game uses words instead of images or shapes, early learners naturally practice reading and recognizing written text

## How to Play

### Starting the Game

1. Launch the app to see the **home screen** with the "Word Sequence" title
2. If you've played before, your current score will be displayed
3. Tap the large blue **"Play"** button to start a round

### Game Play

Each round follows these steps:

1. **Watch carefully** — Words will appear on screen one at a time in different positions and colors
2. **Remember the order** — Pay attention to which word appeared first, second, third, and so on
3. **Wait patiently** — All words will be revealed with a short pause between each one
4. **Tap in sequence** — After all words are shown, the message "Tap in order!" appears
5. **Tap each word** in the exact order they were revealed
   - Correctly tapped words turn **green**
   - If you tap the wrong word, the round ends and shows you which word you should have tapped next

### Winning and Scoring

- **Success** — Tap all words in the correct order to see the "Well Done!" screen with a star
  - Your score increases by 1
  - Tap "Play Again" to continue
  
- **Try Again** — If you tap a word out of order:
  - The game highlights which word you should have tapped
  - Choose "Try Again" to replay the round
  - Choose "Home" to return to the start screen

## Accessing Settings

Settings are designed to be accessible to parents and caregivers while remaining hidden during regular gameplay.

**To open Settings:**
- Tap the **top-left corner** of the screen (an invisible 70×70 point area)
- This hidden tap zone keeps curious young players focused on the game

**To close Settings:**
- Tap the **"Done"** button in the top-right corner of the Settings screen

## Settings Options

### Word List

**What it does:** Customize which words appear in the game to match your child's interests, learning goals, or vocabulary level.

**Default words:** Cat, Dog, Sun, Hat, Car, Tree, Ball, Fish, Bird, Star

**How to use:**
- View all available words in the list
- **Add a new word:**
  1. Type the word in the "Add a word…" text field
  2. Tap "Add" or press Done on the keyboard
- **Remove a word:**
  1. Swipe left on any word in the list
  2. Tap the delete button that appears
- **Edit mode:**
  - Tap "Edit" in the top-left to reorder or delete multiple words
  - Tap "Done" when finished

**Tips:**
- Use familiar words to build confidence
- Add themed words (animals, colors, family members, etc.)
- Words are randomly selected each round, so the game stays fresh
- **Personalize for engagement:** Add names of friends and family, favorite places or locations, or our personal favorite — funny sounding words that get a laugh!
- **Completely customizable:** The default word list can be deleted and replaced entirely with your own words that are meaningful to your child

### Round Settings

#### Words per Round
**Default:** 3 words  
**Range:** 2 to 8 words (or the total number of words in your word list, whichever is smaller)

**What it does:** Controls how many words appear in each round

**How to adjust:**
- Use the **+ and − buttons** to increase or decrease
- Start with fewer words (2-3) for beginners
- Gradually increase as your child's memory and sequencing skills improve

#### Time Between Words
**Default:** 1.5 seconds  
**Range:** 0.1 to 5.0 seconds (adjustable in 0.1 second increments)

**What it does:** Controls the pause duration between each word appearing during the reveal phase

**How to adjust:**
- Drag the **slider** left (faster) or right (slower)
- The current setting is displayed above the slider (e.g., "2.3s")
- Slower speeds (2.0–3.0s) give more time to process each word
- Faster speeds (0.5–1.0s) increase the challenge for advanced players

### Progress

Track your child's achievements and engagement:

- **Score** — Total number of successfully completed rounds
- **Rounds played** — Total rounds attempted (including both successes and failures)

**Reset score button:**
- Tap this red button to reset both Score and Rounds Played to zero
- Useful for starting fresh or for multiple children sharing the device

## Tips for Parents and Caregivers

- **Start small:** Begin with 2 words and slower reveal times, then gradually increase difficulty
- **Celebrate effort:** Progress happens through practice, not just correct answers
- **Personalize:** Use words that are meaningful to your child (pet names, favorite foods, family members)
- **Be patient:** Some days will be easier than others, and that's completely normal
- **Use as routine:** Regular short sessions can help build focus and sequential thinking skills over time
